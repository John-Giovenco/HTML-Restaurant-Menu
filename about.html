Origin Story

We started H-Thai-ML in 2012 at the Bellagio food court when the owner of the Bellagio met the owner at a Chilis and complained about the food. I said to him, "Sir, I will make you better food." The next day I did. A few days later he kicked out his lowest producing restaurant and we took over the location. We've only grown since.
